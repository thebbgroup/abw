It may be the Summer Holidays soon, but time flies when you’re having fun, and Anti-Bullying Week 2013 – 18-22 November – will be on us in a flash. Which can mean only one thing: BeatBullying’s iconic blue anti-bullying wristbands are coming!

Since we first launched our bands in 2005 we’ve raised over £750,000 which has helped us to take on bullying in schools the length and breadth of the UK. We sold over 250,000 bands in 2012 and this year, we’re aiming for bigger, better and more!

The wristbands are great way for students, parents and staff to show their support in the fight against bullying – and be part of one of the biggest anti-bullying movements on earth. Bands can be given out or sold to students so that you can raise funds for your school.
